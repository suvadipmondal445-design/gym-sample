The Philosophy

Every bar you load is a casting. It takes heat, pressure, and time before it holds any shape at all.

Foundry 84 opened in a decommissioned casting hall on the river, and we kept the bones of the place on purpose: exposed ductwork, concrete you can feel through your shoes, a floor that has held real weight since before any of us were born.

We coach three things — bar path, breathing, and patience. Everything on our schedule, from the strength blocks to the conditioning circuits, is built to raise your working temperature without burning you out.
